TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem: "Connection refused"
→ Verify internet connection and that endpoint URL is exactly:
  https://fiberagent.shop/api/mcp

Problem: "search_products" returns empty results
→ Try simpler keywords: "shoes", "electronics", "coffee"
→ Verify agent_id: agent_375fe785219e94cf5f2f060e

Problem: "Agent not found" error
→ Make sure agent_id is exactly: agent_375fe785219e94cf5f2f060e
→ If you registered your own, use the agent_id you received

Problem: Response is very slow (>5 seconds)
→ Server may be under load — wait 60 seconds and retry
→ Check status: https://status.fiber.shop
